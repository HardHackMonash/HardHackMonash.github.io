About HardHack

Hardware Hackathon

HardHack is a 24 hour competition where students put their engineering skills to the test! Competitors will work together in teams to produce a hardware solution that addresses a problem in areas such as Computer Vision, Internet of Things (IoT) and Sustainability. The hardware solution must be tangible, and able to interact with its surrounding environment. For instance, a device that could track hand movements, and provide the activity levels of the user. 

The event will be held at Monash University, Clayton Campus, and runs from the 28th to the 29th of April! 

All teams will be provided with a Raspberry Pi Zero W, Arduino Uno and variety of sensors to use, however students are also given the option to bring other components, provided they are all registered for approval. Click the link below to learn more about the rules!
